    Designed with JBL Hardware

    JBL is a ubiquitous brand in many countries. Alongside Industrial and UI designers, I worked within the Industrial Design team at Harman International in Shenzhen China to take a first stab at the app landscape relevant for a brand like JBL. Over the course of 4 months, I designed 2 applications (the second is no longer maintained). I coached 2 visual designers into crafting modern (at the time) visuals.

    Team: Industrial Design @ Harman International (Shenzhen, China)
    Role: Interaction Design Intern
