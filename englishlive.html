Unique, tailor-made, cross-device, and readable.

My involvement in this project was to leverage the existing English Live platform on desktop Web and rethink the design and interaction patterns of the e-learning templates in a way better suited to mobile platforms. Within these interactions, we also took into consideration our target customers, the existing language barrier, mobile OS design consistency and even 17 supported UI languages. The work covered in EnglishLive for iOS & Android: ideation, sketching, wireframes, user testing & research , visual style exploration, interactive prototyping.

Team: User Experience & Design @ EF Education First (Shanghai, China)
Role: User Experience Designer

EF English Live is available on Google Play and the Apple App Store.
